Why Choose LY Surfboards?

At LY Surfboards we appreciate that no two surfers are the same, that's why we have such a diverse range of models to cater for any style or ability - from beginners boards right through to big guns and tow boards. Even though we use models we are still a 'custom surfboards' business. We tailor every board to each individual customers requirements- also if a customer has an idea of their own we're more than happy to build it for them. With the combination of only the most premium quality materials, second to none craftsmanship and the best possible customer care, we strive for the ultimate service.

About Luke

Now in his late 30s, Luke has over 20 years of "off the blank" specialist shaping under his belt. This experience has lead to highly refined rocker lines, concaves and overall foils of his custom surfboards. All shapes are field tested by the shaper himself as well as team riders in national and international conditions. All abilities and tastes catered for from high performance shortboards to ten foot triple stringer mals, twin fin retro boards to travel boards and tow boards. You name it, we can provide it to the highest possible quality and performance. Machine profile programs are also available to replicate that magic board Luke has been shaping with Brian Bulkley (Bulkley surfboards, Lost surfboards), Hawaii's legendary gun shaper Pat Rawson (Rawson Surfboards Hawaii) and more recently Rob Vaughan of Billabong surfboards Europe.
